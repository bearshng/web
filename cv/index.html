Biography

I join school of computer science and engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology in October 2019 as a lecturer. I received my bachelor degree from school of software, Shandong University, in June, 2014. From 2014 to 2019, I studied in the college of computer science, Zhejiang University where I obtained my Ph.D degree in September 2019. I visited Wuhan University as an exchange student from September, 2011 to July, 2012 and Griffith University as a visiting scholar from November 2017 to November 2018. My research lies in computer vision and hyperspectral imaging. I am a co-receiptent of Best Student Paper Award of DICTA 2020.
